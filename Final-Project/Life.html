Vincent van Gogh


Life of Vincent

  • 1853 - Born in Groot Zundert, Netherlands
  • 1861-1968 - School education. Vincent showed no signs of having talent
  • 1869-1880 - Vincent's first job. He started at Goupil & Cie art dealership group in Hague and after 4 years he was transferred to London
  • 1880 - Vincent goes to Brussels to study art with a financial aid from his brother Theo
  • 1884 - Woved back with parents to Netherlands after years of study and dreams to find love that didn't come true
  • 1885 - Vincent painted "The Potato Eaters". Later this work will be one of his most famous
  • 1886 - Vincent moved to Paris to his brother Theo and met local impressionists
  • 1888 - At the beggining of the year Vincent moved to Arles- South of France. The beauty of the French hinterland will inspire him for his most popular arts including a collection of Sunflowers painting and "Night Cafe"
  • 1888 - In December' Vincent cut off his ear
  • 1889 - Vincent moved to St. Remy for his ear treatment and in this city he painted "Starry Night"- the most famous work of Vincent
  • 1890 - Vincent moved closer to Paris and in the small village Auvers-sur-Oise he started painting his self-portraits
  • 1890 - In July Vincent visited wheatfields to paint them and allegedly shot himself. Van Gogh was burried in Auvers-sur-Oise

Unfortunately Vincent did not become famous during his short life
